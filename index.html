Short Bio

I'll join UNC-Chapel Hill as a Ph.D. student from 2021 spring. And currently, I'm a research intern in the Tencent Youtu Lab.

Prior to that, I completed my Master's degree from UC Davis advised by Yong Jae Lee and Bachelor's degree from Nankai University advised by Ming-Ming Cheng.

I'm broadly interested in Computer Vision and related problems in Machine Learning. And my current research is focused on instance segmentation, object detection, and pedestrian detection.
