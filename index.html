Short Bio

I'm a research intern in the Tencent Youtu Lab and I'm looking for Ph.D. positions starting from 2021.

Prior to that, I completed my Master's degree from UC Davis advised by Yong Jae Lee and Bachelor's degree from Nankai University advised by Ming-Ming Cheng.

I am broadly interested in computer vision and related problems in machine learning. My research experience is focused on instance segmentation, object detection, and pedestrian detection. Currently, I'm particularly interested in self-supervised learning.
